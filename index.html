Our Projects

PiggyFy

PiggyFi solves money problems for Africans using the blockchain.
The main features are easy & automated peer-to-peer money
transfers within and outside Africa, savings in US dollars to
protect against inflation & devaluation and P2P payments
infrastructure for blockchain-powered businesses in Africa.
